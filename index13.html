About Me

I'm a clinical psychologist working in both English and Mandarin. With a background in Applied Psychology (B.A.) and Clinical Psychology (M.A.), I specialize in helping individuals navigate social adaptation difficulties caused by psychological issues‚Äîsuch as emotional disorders, personality disorders, and neurodevelopmental conditions like Autism Spectrum Disorder and ADHD.

I primarily work from a Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) perspective, while integrating elements of psychodynamic and humanistic approaches. My goal is to tailor each therapeutic process to the individual, offering a warm, safe, and respectful space to explore emotions, relationships, and patterns‚Äîtogether finding the path to meaningful change.

Counseling Services

Individual Counseling
Maladaptive social functioning associated with psychological disorders, including generalized anxiety, depressive symptoms, and affective dysregulation.
Family Relationship Counseling
With a focus on issues rooted in early family experiences, exploration of intergenerational family patterns, parent‚Äìchild dynamics, and couple/marital relationships.
Mental Support
Providing psychological support for individuals with severe mental illnesses, such as schizophrenia and bipolar disorder, to enhance coping, emotional stability, and overall functioning.
Adolescent Counseling
Academic pressure, test anxiety, adolescent issues, internet addiction and other psychology problems specific to teenagers.

Counseling Philosophy

"Everyone has a light within. My job is to help you find and ignite it."

I believe counseling is a process of mutual respect and equal cooperation. Here, there is no judgment, only understanding; no standard answers, only solutions that work best for you.
